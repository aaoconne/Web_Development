Final Inning

By choosing to swing at the pitch, you didn't strikout immediately, but that doesn't mean you're in the clear just yet.

Four possible scenarios can play out. Choose one of the options below to see what your swing entails:

  • Hit a dinger over the wall for a grandslam to win the game.

  • Swing at the ball and get a base hit while runner at third advances to win the game

  • Pop fly to get out

  • Hit the ball to shortstop to immediately be thrown out
